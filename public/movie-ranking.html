The Best Movies According to Alex Armas

My top 3 movies of all time.

Interstellar

This movie took my breath away when I saw it in the movie theatres. It really expanded my mind and I left feeling so in awe by the theories and possibilites of the universe.

I Am Legend

This movie with Will Smith was incredible. The whole concept was such fun and exciting story to wrap the head around and put yourself in those shoes. Since then I have thought what I would do if I were the last one standing.

The Hateful Eight

Just an amazing adventure and so uniquely filmed and directed. I loved the tension and twists and how it kept me on my toes. The acting is phenomenal with sucha variety of characters to explore.
